Process

Measured slowly. Finished precisely.

The best garments do not just fit once in front of a mirror. They sit correctly when you walk, host, greet, and move through the evening. Our process is built around that.

01

Consultation

We discuss event type, style direction, fabric mood, and how the garment should feel on the body.

02

Measurement

Precise measurements and fit notes shape the pattern, posture correction, sleeve balance, and fall.

03

Fittings

We refine drape, structure, and finishing through fittings until the garment settles naturally.

04

Delivery

You receive a polished final piece ready for ceremony, celebration, or your everyday professional wardrobe.
